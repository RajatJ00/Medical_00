ITI

ITI stands for Industrial Training Institute, which offers vocational training programs in various trades such as electrician, mechanic, welder, plumber, and carpenter, among others, to prepare individuals for skilled jobs in different industries.

10 + 2

"10 + 2" refers to the educational stage after completing 10 years of schooling (typically secondary education) followed by an additional 2 years (usually higher secondary education), which is a common educational pathway in many countries before pursuing higher education or entering the workforce.

DIPLOMA

Diploma programs offer specialized training and education in a particular field or discipline, typically at a post-secondary level, to prepare students for entry-level positions or further studies in their chosen area of study.
